Company Overview

Since 2015

Urban Design & Construction is a leading design, construction, development, and real estate management company based in Islamabad, Pakistan. As a key player in the development sector, Urban Design & Construction offers residents and businesses in the Twin Cities unparalleled commercial projects and designer residences that blend luxury, style, and practicality like never before. Our nationwide services encompass top-tier architectural design, interior design, commercial project design, and cutting-edge construction. We bring your dream residential and commercial projects to life, transforming ideas and resources into architectural masterpieces.

  • Top-tier architectural design and interior services
  • Innovative residential and commercial project design
  • Innovative construction with a focus on durability and efficiency

Mission and Vission

Urban design and construction was the brainchild of Chairman Malik Tayyab Awan whose idea was later materialized by CEO Owais Bajwa. Despite having a successful career in Engineering, the CEO set out to realize his vision of creating designer villas, residential projects and commercial projects. The company focuses on catering to the needs of its customers by creating artistic designs.. Our company comprises of teams that fully comprehend the need of time and desires of the customers, which is, reflected in our state-of-the-art projects. Our projects are characterized by wide spaces, front elevation, and facades as well as cost-effective construction. We stay committed from the start of the planning process to the end, delivering you extraordinary results. We provide exceptional service, exceptional design and exceptional property. Moreover, we offer property management and end-to-end real estate services covering bulk lands, residential and commercial plots, industrial areas, housing and residential services concerning purchase, sale, lease and appraisal for all Pakistanis inside and outside Pakistan and to all foreigners.
